## How to subscribe

dunedinsound.com practices [POSSE](https://indieweb.org/POSSE) (Publish Own Site, Syndicate Elsewhere).

If you want to receive notifications when new gigs are added while avoiding corporate social media there's a few ways you can do this.

### RSS

You can subscribe to this sites RSS feed by installing a [feed reader](https://indieweb.org/feed_reader). These are useful for keeping up with things like podcasts and blogs without having to sign-up for anything.

[Feeder](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.nononsenseapps.feeder.play) is a good choice for Android, but many options exist.

After that, clicking here should add it to your feed reader:

<a title="Subscribe to RSS feed" href="/rss.xml">
  <RssIcon width="50px" height="50px" aria-label="Subscribe to RSS feed" />
</a>

### Fediverse

This site is bridged to the [fediverse](https://indieweb.org/federation). This means updates are spat out into various federated services such as [Mastodon](https://mastodon.social/).

You can follow dunedinsound.com on ActivityPub compatible services (Mastodon) with this handle: `@dunedinsound.com@dunedinsound.com`

Or on ATProto compatible services (Bluesky) with this handle: `dunedinsound.com.web.brid.gy`
